Research

Undergraduate Thesis

Automatic System for Detecting Invasive Ductal Carcinoma Using Convolutional Neural Networks

The goal of our research was to create an automated system that will analyze the whole mount slide images of breast cancer specimens to indicate the exact positions of Invasive ductal carcinoma (IDC) inside of the slides and provide decisions based on the results. The research achieved an accuracy of 89.34 in f1 score using convolutional neural network.
